Signs Your Drain May Need Repairs

As you probably know, it is always better to fix the drain problems when they start to appear. If you postpone fixing the problem, it will only get worse. A fully clogged drain is more difficult – and more costly – to fix. Here are some of the signs and symptoms you need expert drain cleaning or repair services in Long Island:

Persistent slow drainage
Unpleasant sewer odors
Persistent clogs throughout the house
Wastewater leaking
Increase in water bill
Backflow problems
Plunger is of no use

As a recommendation, you should consider cleaning the drains every 18 to 22 months. This ensures the drains are in their best shape.
